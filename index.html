What I do

I create digital spaces, to access and experience data, but also to create a modern and interactive learning environment.

More about me

Educated as a teacher in geography and with a lot of work experience from the social sector, I made it my goal to prepare learning and data content in a digital, interactive and visually appealing way.

The use of (open) data in the classroom has received (too) little attention so far. Digital and interactive applications make complex issues in the classroom tangible and understandable. Visualizations that present real and concrete data sets can be used, for example, to discuss technical questions from politics, social studies and geography lessons with direct reference to the immediate environment.
